Heritance Kandalama


The distinctive Heritance Kandalama hotel, built into a cliff face amidst dense tropical trees and flanked by the Dambulla Rock Temple and Sigiriya Rock Fortress, was created by renowned Sri Lankan architect Geoffrey Bawa. The hotel's structures are covered in flowering plants and hanging vines in an effort to blend in with the surroundings.The Kandalama is a miracle of architecture and is located in the midst of Sri Lanka's cultural triangle. The eighth wonder of the world, Sigiriya, is visible from this hotel, which is carved into the mountainside in the shape of a bird with its wings spread.



Heritance Tea Factory


Which region in the world offers accommodations in a tea plant that has been transformed into a five-star hotel? Moreover, you are allowed to collect your own tea to take home as a memento. This is what the newly renovated Hotel Hertance Tea Factory in the "Little England" region of Nuwara Eliya has to offer. Tea Factory, which is 2,000 metres above sea level, provides vistas of the verdant plantations, where the vision of the sun through the fog is absolutely remarkable. You can take pleasure in them when sipping tea on the patio or while dining on a delectable menu in a train car.Everybody requires a place to rest their aching head. Heritance Tea Factory is a great place to unwind and recharge for visitors to Nuwara Eliya. Heritance Tea Factory is renowned for its kid-friendly atmosphere and close proximity to fantastic eateries, which makes it simple to enjoy the finest of Nuwara Eliya.



Heritance Aarah


In the upscale Heritance Aarah Lounge in Male, you can wait for a 40-minute beautiful seaplane flight. The Premium All-Inclusive resort in the Maldives, which is tucked away within the picturesque Raa Atoll, creates the ideal retreat with a wide range of amenities, from dining at an astonishing variety of restaurants prepared by award-winning chefs to enjoying the above- and below-water wonders of the Raa Atoll.
