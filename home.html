Heritance Hotels and Resorts


The Heritance brand enhances travel destinations in Sri Lanka and beyond by highlighting each place's cultural heritage. Heritance Hotels and Resorts, a member of the Aitken Spence Hotels group, is one of twenty-two hotels and resorts located in Sri Lanka, India, the Maldives, and Oman, among other locations in South Asia and the Middle East.

Our unparalleled hospitality is a source of immense pride for us, and it will forever be intertwined with our reputation - no matter where our paths may cross on your extraordinary journeys.

Every corner of our properties showcases our deep-rooted values and passion for Sri Lanka, inspiring you to explore this beautiful country. Discover its hidden gems and create treasured memories, all infused with the vibrant spirit of its people, places, and captivating stories.
