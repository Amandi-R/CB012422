Adventures


HeritanceÂ offers a wide range of activities. Make sure to check out the fun activities that are offered at the hotel, as they will make your stay even more enjoyable.




Cycling


For your safety and comfort, we provide off-road bicycles for rent that are ideally suited to the challenging terrain and are routinely inspected and maintained. If you are interested in renting a bike, kindly inquire at reception.

You can follow the trails on the map we give. Cycling around Kandalama Lake is one route. The lake's water levels fluctuate throughout the year, occasionally exposing significant sand and mud patches and occasionally isolating islands in the water.



Tea Plucking


How about bringing home a packet of tea that you choose yourself as the perfect holiday souvenir? We will dress you in traditional garb at the Heritance Tea Factory, including a saree for women and a sarong for men, and give you a basket to harvest your leaves in.

Then we'll take you outside to our organic tea plantations and demonstrate how to pick the right leaves. Making sure the leaves land in your basket as opposed to on the ground will take some getting used to. You'll be astounded at how fast and effectively our trained tea pluckers manage to fill their baskets in comparison when they accompany you. Fortunately, we don't anticipate you to meet their aim of producing at least 15 kg every day!



Snorkeling


The Maldives are unquestionably a paradise for snorkelers and divers alike, whether you intend to visit Hammerhead Point or take part in a turtle hunt. The nation boasts one of the best underwater environments in the world and is home to a variety of incredible marine life.
