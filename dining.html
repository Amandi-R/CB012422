Dine With Us


We provide a wide variety of en suite dining alternatives and are always ready to accommodate your every need with unmatched attention to detail. This, in our opinion, is what genuinely distinguishes us from other hotels.







Jute Restaurant


You'll be equally tempted as you enter the Jute Restaurant by exotic scents and tranquil garden views. In this restaurant, which is known for its lavish buffets, you may indulge in gourmet cuisine from all over the world, day or night. This is our main restaurant, which has the name of the batik ceiling and serves filling breakfasts, sumptuous lunches, and thrilling themed dinners to satisfy even the most voracious appetites.



TYPES OF DINING : International cuisines and theme nights

Cafe Kachchan


The first location you visit upon arrival and the spot to hangout at any time after, Cafe Kachchan overlooks the infinity pool and offers expansive views of the Kandalama Lake and Sigirya Rock Fortress. From seven in the morning until midnight, every day, a variety of snacks, drinks, and bites are available.



TYPES OF DINING : A la Carte

See The Menu






Kanchana Restaurant


Our major dining space is the Kanchana Restaurant, which is located high on the sixth level and has full-length glass windows that provide panoramic views. Breakfast, lunch, and supper spreads tempt you with a variety of cuisine, from traditional Sri Lankan food to cosmopolitan fare. As dusk falls, a flute plays enchanting tunes while the stars shine and a magnificent BBQ sizzles.



TYPES OF DINING : International Cuisine, Buffet or A la Carte
